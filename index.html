Inferhythm helps you find the Genius page for the song you're currently listening to on Spotify.

How Inferhythm Works

  1. Start by playing any track on Spotify.

  2. Click the extension icon to the right of the address bar.

  3. After a few seconds, a list of Genius results will show up.

  4. Click on any of the results to open it in a new tab.

Requirements

A Spotify account is required.

You will need to grant Inferhythm permission access to read your Spotify's recently played tracks.
